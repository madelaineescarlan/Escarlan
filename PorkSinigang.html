Pork Sinigang
A classic Filipino sour and savory soup that features pork ribs, vegetables, and tamarind-flavored broth.





Pork Sinigang Recipe

  • 2 pounds pork liempo, cut 2 inches chunks
  • 1 package tamarind sinigang soup mix
  • 1 tomato, quartered
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 1 taro(gabi), quartered
  • 3-4 long green chili peppers (siling haba)
  • 1 medium size radish (labanos), sliced
  • 3 string beans (sitaw), cut in 2 inches long
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 10 stems of kangkong leaves
  • fish sauce, to taste

Directions for Cooking

  1. Rinse pork ribs and drain well.
  2. Boil water in a pot over medium heat.
  3. Lower the heat and Add the pork liempo, tomato, onion, and tamarind sinigang soup mix.
  4. Cover the pot and cook for about an hour to an hour and a half or until meat is tender.
  5. Add the long chili pepper, radish, taro, string beans, and eggplant.
  6. Cover the pot and continue boiling until the vegetables are cooked.
  7. Add the kangkong leaves and simmer until the dish is done.
  8. Season with fish sauce preference to your taste.
  9. Remove from heat, Serve and Enjoy!
