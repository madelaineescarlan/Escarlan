Chicken Adobo
A favorite Filipino dish that features chickens that are marinated in soy sauce,
vinegar and spices, pan-fried, and stewed until tender.





Chicken Adobo Recipe

  • 2 pounds chicken thighs or drumsticks
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 3/4 cup white vinegar
  • 3/4 cup soy sauce
  • 8 cloves garlic crushed
  • 2 chilies of jalapenos sliced (divided)
  • 2 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 bay leaves

Directions for Cooking

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large pan/skillet, heat oil in medium to high heat.
  3. When the oil is hot enough, Add the chicken side down to the pan and cook until brown on both sides.
  4. Remove the chicken from the pan and place on a plate.
  5. To the pan, add the vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, half the chilies, black pepper, brown sugar, and bay leaves.
  6. Boil them in medium to high heat.
  7. Add the chicken, skin side up, reduce heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for 30-35 minutes until cooked through and tender.
  8. Remove chicken from pan. Bring sauce to a boil in medium to high heat and cook for about 5 minutes or until slightly thickened.
  9. Remove the pan from the heat, discard bay leaves, and add the chicken back. Spoon sauce over the top of the chicken and garnish with remaining chilies.
  10. Serve and Enjoy!
